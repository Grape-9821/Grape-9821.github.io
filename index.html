Global Inventors

Thomas Edison

Invented the practical light bulb, phonograph, and motion picture camera. He held over 1,000 patents and transformed electricity use.

Nikola Tesla

Pioneered alternating current (AC) electricity and wireless communication, shaping modern electrical systems.

Leonardo da Vinci

Renaissance genius known for his art and visionary inventions like flying machines and armored vehicles.

Alexander Graham Bell

Invented the telephone, changing communication, and made contributions to hearing aids and aeronautics.

Marie Curie

Discovered radioactivity, won two Nobel Prizes, and advanced cancer treatment with radiation technology.
